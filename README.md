# Workforce Intelligence & HR Attrition Analytics Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project is a professional HR Analytics Dashboard developed in Power BI to analyze employee attrition, workforce behavior, and organizational stability. The dashboard converts raw HR data into actionable business insights that help management and HR teams make data-driven decisions.

The project focuses on identifying key attrition drivers such as overtime, travel frequency, job satisfaction, work-life balance, and department-level workforce instability.

---

# Dashboard Objectives

- Analyze employee attrition trends
- Identify high-risk departments and job roles
- Monitor workforce satisfaction and engagement
- Evaluate the impact of overtime and business travel
- Support strategic HR decision-making
- Improve employee retention planning

# Key Business Insights

- Employees working overtime show significantly higher attrition rates.
- Frequent business travel strongly impacts employee retention.
- Low satisfaction and poor work-life balance increase resignation probability.
- Technical and operational departments experience higher workforce instability.
- Mid-career employees represent the highest attrition risk segment.

---

# Department-Level Insights

Highest attrition observed in:
- Cyber Security
- Software Development
- Data Science
- IT Services

---

# Strategic Recommendations

## 1. Role-Specific Retention Programs
Develop targeted retention strategies for high-risk technical and operational roles.

## 2. Workforce Fatigue Reduction
Reduce excessive overtime and improve work-life balance policies.

## 3. Predictive HR Monitoring
Implement employee risk scoring and proactive workforce monitoring systems.

## 4. Employee Engagement Strategy
Strengthen recognition programs, career development, and leadership support.

## 5. Executive Workforce Intelligence
Enable management with real-time HR analytics dashboards for strategic decision-making.
